Day 2 Events

DASH OF TRIVIA


Round 1 - TECH-TIAN: Teams of two tackle multiple-choice questions, puzzles, and riddles within a time limit.
Round 2 - HACK O HUNT (Treasure Hunt): Participants follow clues to navigate locations, gather information, and solve a problem statement.

MR.WORKSHOP


Round 1 - BRAND BRILL: Participants identify car and bike brand logos within a 20-minute time limit.
Round 2 - WORKSHOPGEEK: Shortlisted participants answer workshop-based questions specific to their academic year.
Round 3 - WORKSHOP RIZZ: Finalists present a product based on a given design using workshop or lathe tools.

IDEA EXCHANGE


Participants (non-IBT) choose a topic on innovations in biology, engineering, or industrial biotech and present a 10-12 slide PPT to judges.

TECH TREK


Round 1 - APTI-QUEST: Participants solve general aptitude questions covering quantitative reasoning, logic, and verbal skills.
Round 2 - TECH-TRIVIA: Teams face technical questions on programming, algorithms, and data structures.
Round 3 - CODE CLASH: Participants tackle competitive programming problems in real-time to test coding skills.
