Day 3 Events

SPEAK UP


Round 1 - Group Discussion: Participants discuss a given topic, focusing on respectful, concise, and relevant communication. (35-minute limit)
Round 2 - Public Speaking: Selected participants present on a topic of their choice, engaging the audience while maintaining relevance and respect. (2 minutes per speaker)
Round 3 - Debate: Finalists engage in 1-on-1 debates on a given topic, emphasizing respectful and on-topic arguments. (5 minutes per participant)

BUILDING VISIONS


Round 1 - Discover the Icons: Participants identify historical monuments displayed on the screen by writing their names within 30 seconds per question.
Round 2 - Blueprints in Graphite: Shortlisted teams design buildings based on a provided theme. Second-year students use pencils on drawing sheets, while third-year students use AutoCAD software. (60-minute limit)

SPECBUILD


Round 1 - Tech-Wiz Quiz: Participants answer 15-20 questions on OOPs, software engineering basics, SRS documentation, and modular programming within 15 minutes.
Round 2 - Spec-Doc Crafters: Shortlisted teams create an SRS document based on a specified theme, evaluated on creativity and accuracy.
Round 3 - Blueprint Architects: Teams design a basic software architecture for solutions specified in their SRS document, requiring a laptop with stable WiFi.

TECHNEX


Round 1 - Electroniq: Participants answer 25 questions covering electrical engineering, electronics, and IoT fundamentals.
Round 2 - Design-O-Logic: Teams build a circuit or simple project using TinkerCad. A laptop with a stable network connection is recommended.
